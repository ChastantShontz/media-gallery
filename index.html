The first major conflict between the Jedi and Sith was the Great Hyperspace War. For one hundred years before this, the Sith Empire lived in isolation under the stable rule of Marka Ragnos. However, following Ragnos's death, a power struggle erupted between rival Sith Lords Naga Sadow and Ludo Kressh. The discovery of the Sith homeworld of Korriban by hyperspace scouts Gav and Jori Daragon brought the Empire into contact with the Galactic Republic, and an invasion led by Sadow commenced. Despite his usage of battle meditation to bolster his forces, Sadow was ultimately defeated by Empress Teta. His flagship crash landed on Yavin 4, where he lived out the remainder of his life in exile.

After almost three decades of war between the Galactic Republic and the Sith Empire, the conflict was brough to an end with the Sacking of Coruscant. Mentally influenced by the imprisoned Jedi Revan, the Sith Emperor decided to vie for peace. While the Republic was distracted with the negotiations on Alderaan, the Sith Lord Darth Malgus led an attack on the Jedi Temple on Coruscant, decimating it. Holding Coruscant hostage, the Empire was then able to force the Republic into signing the unfavorable Treaty of Coruscant. Under these terms, half of the galaxy was handed over to the Empire, ushering in a new age of Cold War.
