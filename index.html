The first major conflict between the Jedi and Sith was the Great Hyperspace War. For one hundred years before this, the Sith Empire lived in isolation under the stable rule of Marka Ragnos. However, following Ragnos's death, a power struggle erupted between rival Sith Lords Naga Sadow and Ludo Kressh. The discovery of the Sith homeworld of Korriban by hyperspace scouts Gav and Jori Daragon brought the Empire into contact with the Galactic Republic, and an invasion led by Sadow commenced. Despite his usage of battle meditation to bolster his forces, Sadow was ultimately defeated by Empress Teta. His flagship crash landed on Yavin 4, where he lived out the remainder of his life in exile.

After almost three decades of war between the Galactic Republic and the Sith Empire, the conflict was brough to an end with the Sacking of Coruscant. Mentally influenced by the imprisoned Jedi Revan, the Sith Emperor decfdescrip ed to vie for peace. While the Republic was distracted with the negotiations on Alderaan, the Sith Lord Darth Malgus led an attack on the Jedi Temple on Coruscant, decimating it. Holding Coruscant hostage, the Empire was then able to force the Republic into signing the unfavorable Treaty of Coruscant. Under these terms, half of the galaxy was handed over to the Empire, ushering in a new era of Cold War.

The Sith Lord Darth Bane orchestrated the destruction of the flawed Brotherhood of Darkness using the thought bomb. This was a complex ritual that instantly killed all Force-sensitive beings within its radius, including the ones who created it. After one thousand years of constant warfare between the Jedi and Sith, culminating in a two-year-long campaign on Ruusan, both sides were on the brink of destruction. Bane, who had grown disillusioned with the Brotherhood's philosophy of all Sith being equals, manipulated its leader Skere Kaan into using the thought bomb against the Army of Light. Afterward, as the sole surviving Sith, Bane established the Rule of Two, which dictated that there could be no more than two Sith at a time: a master and an apprentice.

The most descructive conflict in galactic history was the Yuuzhan Vong invasion. The Yuuzhan Vong were alien refugees from another galaxy, which was largely destroyed in terrible civil war. For fifteen thousand years, they wanted between galaxies looking for a new one to call home. When they finally settled on a galaxy to invade, the once hostile New Republic and Imperial Remnant were forced into an uneasy alliance to combat them. Victory was only achieved with help from the sentient extra-galactic planet Zonama Sekot, the seed of the Vong's own homeworld Yuuzhan'tar. Supreme Overlord Shimrra Jamaane was killed by Luke Skywalker, and the Vong were exiled from the galaxy onboard Sekot.
